Meet Your PGA Professional

Richard Mudge

Northamptonshire PGA Teaching Professional

With years of experience and a passion for golf, Richard Mudge is dedicated to helping golfers of all abilities improve their game. As a Class AA Level III PGA Coach, Richard offers personalized instruction tailored to your unique swing and goals.

Whether you're a beginner looking to learn the fundamentals or an experienced player aiming to fine-tune your skills, Richard provides expert advice and a supportive learning environment.

Richard specializes in both individual coaching and group lessons, utilizing advanced technology including detailed ball flight analysis, HMT, and GC2 launch monitor to help you understand and perfect your game.
